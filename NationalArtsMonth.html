National Arts Month is celebrated annually throughout February in the
Philippines. This is to promote and provide areas for artists to bring out
their creative energies.

National Arts Month can also be humanities Month. N.C.C.A known as
"The National Commission for Culture and the Arts" oversees and

History of National Arts Month

History of National Arts Month Also refered to as "Ani ng Sining." It goes back to 1991 when President Corazon Aquino named Ferbruary "National Arts Month." it wasn’t until 1992 — through the enactment of “Republic Act 7356” — that the Commission was formally established. The N.C.C.A is the government body charged with the responsibility of policymaking and implementation, grant-giving, and coordination, concerning preserving, promoting, and developing Philippine culture and arts. The official vision of the Commission is: “A Filipino people with a strong sense of nationhood and deep respect for cultural diversity.”

National Arts and Humanities Month Goals

F.E.A.R! I know it seems scary but I promise it's not!

FOCUSING - To create a national, state and local focus on the arts and humanities through the media

ENCOURAGING - To encourage the participation of individuals, as well as arts, humanities and other organizations nationwide

ALLOWING - To provide an opportunity for federal, state and local business, government and civic leaders to declare their support for the arts and humanities

RAISING - To establish a highly visible vehicle for raising public awareness about the arts and humanities.
